## MARKET INTELLIGENCE SUMMARY

### Marine — Nordic/Baltic
- Strong trend toward **electrification** (battery hybrid) and **hydrogen fuel cells** for new builds
- Smaller ferry retrofits (<800 kW) exist but are rarely publicised in English-language sources
- **Methanol retrofit kits** (FASTWATER program, 200 kW–4 MW range) represent a viable Proventia opportunity for ferry operators seeking fuel flexibility
- Norway's electrification of 164 ferry routes creates **aftermarket service and retrofit opportunity** on existing diesel vessels
- **ABB propulsion drive retrofits** (Suomenlinna II reference) indicate OEM retrofit channel opportunity

### Marine — EU Inland Waterways
- **Hydrogen is the dominant retrofit theme** in EU inland waterways (FLAGSHIPS, RH2IWER, ELEKTRA)
- **Methanol** is second (FASTWATER) — proven retrofit kits exist for 200 kW+
- EU funding channels (Horizon Europe, CEF, Interreg, Clean Hydrogen Partnership) heavily subsidise retrofits — operators more likely to proceed with public funding
- Zollverein/Canal barge sector in Germany is a dense cluster of retrofit activity

### Stationary Power
- **Hospital backup** is the strongest sub-vertical: Austrian, Finnish, and broader EU hospital tender activity with units in 300–800 kW range
- **Data centres** are active but typically >1 MW per unit — 500 kW hydrogen (NorthC) and <1MW UPS projects are exceptions
- **Dual-fuel retrofit kits** (UNICEF RFP for 250 kVA) represent a growing market
- **HVO fuel transition** for existing gensets is the low-cost entry point — "retrofit" scope but fuel-only change

### NRMM — UK (strongest market signals)
- **HS2 is the single largest documented NRMM retrofit programme** in UK: Balfour Beatty VINCI saved £50M retrofitting 19 machines rather than replacing
- **Eminox EMx S5** is the first RAS-approved retrofit product — Stage IIIA → Stage V equivalence; a proven compliance pathway
- **Hydrogen ICE** (ULEMCo H2ICED®) is production-ready for piling rigs; dual-fuel (HyConstruct) is in field testing
- UK government **£6.7M Red Diesel Replacement Competition** (16 projects) signals policy-driven retrofit market
- Stage V deadline has passed (Jan 2022) — retrofit market is driven by compliance enforcement on live sites

### NRMM — Finland/Sweden
- **Electric new builds** dominate (Volvo CE, LiuGong, Skanska fossil-free sites) — less retrofit activity
- **Avesco Cat Certified Rebuilds** is the model for engine/hydraulic retrofits with emission compliance
- Rail maintenance machines (Geismar AGX kit) are a niche but active retrofit vertical
